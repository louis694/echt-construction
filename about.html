About Echt

“Echt” means genuine. That is the standard we hold ourselves to — in every pour, every frame, every finish.

Our Story

Founded in 2004, Echt Construction began with a single crew and a straightforward belief: that great construction is the result of honest work, disciplined process, and an uncompromising eye for detail.

Twenty years later, we have delivered over 350 projects across the Pacific Northwest — from custom residential estates to major commercial developments — without once sacrificing quality for speed or budget.
